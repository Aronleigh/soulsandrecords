Soul Sand Records is an independent label built around immersive, conceptual and experience-driven music projects. We release creative worlds — not market categories — and support artists whose work is designed to be lived inside, not simply consumed.

Projects over genres

We focus on complete artistic projects and creative worlds. Sound, concept, identity and experience are developed together.

All genres belong here

Ambient, cinematic, experimental, folk, pop, hip-hop, jazz, country and everything in between are welcome under the same philosophy.

Designed experiences

Some projects are emotional. Some are functional. Some are narrative. All releases are built as intentional listening environments.
