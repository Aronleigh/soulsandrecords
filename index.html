Soul Sand Records is an independent, project-driven label releasing work across all genres. We curate creative worlds — not categories — and support artists whose projects are built around intention, experience, and identity.

Our philosophy

Projects, not genres

We sign creative projects and artistic worlds — not styles, trends, or market categories. Every release stands on its own concept and purpose.

All music is welcome

Hip-hop, country, pop, jazz, folk, Americana, ambient, experimental, cinematic and beyond — Soul Sand Records is intentionally genre-agnostic.

Experience comes first

Each project is developed as an experience — whether emotional, functional, narrative, immersive, or exploratory.
